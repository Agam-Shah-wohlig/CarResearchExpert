Summary The Skoda Slavia is a subcompact sedan manufactured mainly for the Indian market since 2022. It comes with two turbo petrol engine options: a 1.0L TSI and a 1.5L TSI. The Slavia is known for its sleek design, safety features, and performance, with sportier versions like the Monte Carlo and Sportline adding to its appeal. It has a 5-star Global NCAP safety rating. The Skoda Octavia is a small family car produced since 1996 and is Skoda's most popular model. The current generation offers a range of derivatives, including the sporty Octavia RS, the Octavia Combi estate, the four-wheel-drive Octavia Scout, and the CNG-powered Octavia G-TEC.
